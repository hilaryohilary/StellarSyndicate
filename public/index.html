OUR STORY

The Black Eye Galaxy millions of light years away from the Milky Way had been in perfect harmony until a certain mafia got banded - the STELLAR SYNDICATE.

Made up of the worst species in the galaxy, they wreaked havoc all through the galaxy until the Galactic Liberation Force (GLF) was formed against them.

After 12 millienia of hunting down the Stellar Syndicate, the GLF purged the galaxy of these bandits and placed a STELLAR BOUNTY on the extant scoundrels of the syndicate left.

The current dire situation had the last three surviving species of the Stellar Syndicate - the Hirocans, Splexians and Uvians evacuate into our metaverse.
