What are StellarSyndicateNFTs?

StellarSyndicate is a collection of 10 000 algo generated aliens set out to conquer the solana metaverse. Hand drawn by skilled and talented digital artists

So Why Aliens?

Why not? Aliens are fascinating, a myth thus a blank space in our minds filled with creative narratives. We don’t know whether there are any aliens or not. But there are so many planets in the universe that many scientists think it’s worth looking. The problem is that figuring out what aliens will be like isn’t so easy. We have only one example of life – life on Earth – to learn from. One fear in the research on aliens is a possible backfire if they felt threatened and invaded us. The alien invasion or space invasion is a common feature in science fiction stories and film, in which extraterrestrials invade the Earth either to exterminate and supplant human life, enslave it under an intense state, harvest people for food, steal the planet's resources, or destroy the planet altogether. Thus the STELLARSYNDICATE idea was born. A rogue dangerous armed group of aliens set out to conquer the metaverse. The future is in danger.
